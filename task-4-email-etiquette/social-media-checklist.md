# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

## Introduction
Social media is widely used by students for communication, networking, and sharing information. However, it is important to use these platforms responsibly to maintain a positive digital identity and ensure online safety. The following checklist highlights important do's and don'ts for responsible social media use.

---

## ✅ Do's

- Maintain a professional and respectful online profile.
- Think before posting or sharing any content online.
- Use strong and secure passwords for all social media accounts.
- Verify information before sharing it with others.
- Protect your personal information and privacy settings.

---

## ❌ Don'ts

- Do not share sensitive personal information such as phone numbers, addresses, or financial details.
- Avoid posting offensive, inappropriate, or harmful content.
- Do not click on suspicious links or unknown attachments.
- Avoid spreading rumors or unverified information.
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or online harassment.

---

## Conclusion
Responsible social media use helps maintain a positive digital reputation and ensures a safer online environment for everyone. By following these do's and don'ts, students can use social media in a productive and ethical manner.
