# Stay Safe Online – Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

Cybercrime incidents such as phishing, identity theft, and online payment fraud are increasing rapidly. Following these safety practices can help students and internet users protect themselves from online threats.

## General Online Safety Tips

- Use strong and unique passwords for all online accounts.
- Enable two-factor authentication whenever possible.
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or unknown attachments.
- Do not share personal information on untrusted websites.
- Always verify website URLs before entering login details.
- Keep your devices and software updated for security protection.

## UPI and Financial Safety Tips

- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone.
- Always check payment requests carefully before approving them.

## Reporting Cybercrime

If you become a victim of cybercrime or notice suspicious online activity, report it immediately through the official channels:

National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930

## Conclusion

Staying aware of cyber threats and following safe online practices can significantly reduce the risk of cybercrime. Responsible digital behavior helps create a safer online environment for everyone.
